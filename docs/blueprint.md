# **App Name**: LegiFlow

## Core Features:

- Document Parsing: Parse uploaded legal documents into individual clauses using AI. The user will be able to paste documents.
- Plain Language Summaries: Rewrites legal clauses in plain, accessible language; the user will be able to toggle between legal and simplified versions. The AI tool will consider clarity and readability during rewriting.
- Risk and Obligation Highlighting: Identifies and highlights risks and obligations within the legal document, with color-coded tagging (high, medium, safe).
- Timeline and Key Term Extraction: Automatically extract key dates, deadlines, renewal clauses, parties involved, and financial amounts, and displays the extracted information in a simple timeline view.
- Interactive Q&A: Chatbot assistant enabling users to ask questions about the contract and receive plain language explanations.
- Multi-Perspective View: Presents clause meanings from different perspectives, such as Tenant vs. Landlord, or Borrower vs. Lender.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2E5266) to convey trust and authority in the legal context.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F7), offering a clean, neutral backdrop for readability.
- Accent color: Soft orange (#D4A373) to highlight critical information and interactive elements, drawing user attention.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for its modern, neutral look suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear, minimalist icons to represent different document sections and risk levels.
- A clean and intuitive layout, ensuring easy navigation and a seamless user experience.
